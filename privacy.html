Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

Devotion Prayer Journal is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address – used for authentication and friend/group identification.
  • Display Name - used for personalization within the app.
  • Prayer Posts – personal prayer content you submit for yourself, friends, or groups.
  • Device type, app version, and usage logs may be collected by Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics to help improve app performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize app features.
  • Facilitate prayer sharing with friends and groups.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information:

  • With Firebase (Google) as our cloud backend service.
  • If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or legal process.
  • To investigate or prevent fraudulent or harmful activity.

Your email, display name, and prayer posts may be visible to your approved friends and group members within the app.

4. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your display name at any time.
  • You can archive or delete your own prayer posts.
  • You can remove friends and leave groups at your discretion.
  • You can delete your account at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices, including encrypted data transmission and Firebase Authentication for login. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app or on our website with a revised "Effective Date".

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, please email us at: devotional.prayerjournal@gmail.com
